We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data with third parties only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Lead Continuity LLC, our clients, or the public.
  • With Your Consent: When you provide explicit consent to share your information with a specific third party.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. 7. Data Retention

  • Contact Form Submissions: Retained for up to 12 months for correspondence purposes, then deleted.
  • Client Data: Retained for the duration of the active engagement. Upon termination, client data is exported within 5 business days and deleted from our servers within 30 days of written confirmation of receipt.
  • SMS Consent Records: Retained for as long as consent is active and for up to 4 years after opt-out for compliance purposes.
  • Log Data: Retained for up to 90 days for security and debugging purposes.

8. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Infrastructure hosted on reputable cloud providers with SOC 2 compliance.

No electronic transmission or storage method is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
